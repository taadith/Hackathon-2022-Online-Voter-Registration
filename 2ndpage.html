Classification - Voter Registration Application

All fields marked with an asterisk(*) are required

Classification

I am one of the following*

  • (A) A U.S. citizen and resident of California
  • (B) A U.S. citizen and a member of the Uniformed Services or Merchant Marine on active duty outside my county.
  • (C) A U.S. citizen and an eligible spouse or dependent of a member of the Uniformed Services or Merchant Marine on active duty outside my county.
  • (D) A U.S. citizen and an activated National Guard member on State orders outside my county.
  • (E) A U.S. citizen residing outside the U.S. temporarily.
  • (F) A U.S. citizen residing outside the U.S. indefinitely.
  • (G) A U.S. citizen and have never resided in the U.S.
